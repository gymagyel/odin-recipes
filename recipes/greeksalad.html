Greek Salad

Description

Greek salad is a refreshing, colorful Mediterranean dish made with crisp vegetables, olives, and feta cheese. It is perfect as a side dish or a light summer meal.

Ingredients

  • 3 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 100 g Kalamata olives
  • 150 g feta cheese, cubed
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and chop tomatoes, cucumber, onion, and bell pepper.
  2. Place vegetables in a large bowl, add olives and feta cheese.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk olive oil, vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour dressing over the vegetables and gently mix.
  5. Enjoy immediately as a side dish or light main meal.
