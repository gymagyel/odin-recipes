Pancake

Description

Fluffy pancakes are a classic breakfast favorite. They are quick and easy to make, soft on the inside, and golden on the outside. You can serve them with syrup, fruit, or even chocolate spread.

Ingredients

  • 200 g all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 egg
  • 250 ml milk
  • 2 tbsp melted butter or oil
  • Butter/oil for frying

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together egg, milk, and melted butter.
  3. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and mix gently (donâ€™t overmix; batter should be slightly lumpy).
  4. Heat a non-stick pan, grease lightly, and pour small amounts of batter. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden.
  5. Stack pancakes and serve warm with syrup, fruits, or toppings of your choice.
